Project Overview

Management Information Systems (MIS) play a critical role in enabling leadership teams to monitor performance, identify trends, and make informed decisions across business functions.

This MIS dashboard was designed to consolidate data from multiple operational sources into a structured, repeatable reporting layer, ensuring consistency, accuracy, and clarity for management consumption.
