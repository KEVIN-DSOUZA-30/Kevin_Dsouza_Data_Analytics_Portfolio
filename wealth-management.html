Project Overview

Mutual fund audits traditionally rely on manual, Excel-based validation across brokerage, dividend, redemption, TER, and valuation checks. This automation framework independently replicates client-side system logic to enable full-population validation with complete audit traceability.

Automation Objectives

Brokerage Commission Validation
Dividend Reconciliation & Ex-Date Logic
Redemption NAV & T+3 Compliance
TER Slab Compliance (SEBI)
Security Valuation Cross-Verification
